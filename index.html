About Weather Alerts

NOAA Weather Radio is the most reliable way to receive alerts — not outdoor sirens. Sirens are outdoor warning systems designed for people outside, not a personal alert system. Download the WEA (Wireless Emergency Alerts) system on your phone as a secondary source.

Watch vs Warning: A Watch means conditions are possible. A Warning means a threat has been detected. Warnings mean take action now.

About tornado warnings: Tornadoes are relatively small, but warning polygons are larger to account for uncertainty in the storm's path. This doesn't mean "most warnings are false alarms" — it means the warning area is intentionally broad for safety.
