Understanding Alerts

NOAA Weather Radio is the most reliable way to receive alerts — not outdoor sirens. Sirens are outdoor warning systems designed for people outside, not a personal alert system. Enable WEA (Wireless Emergency Alerts) on your phone as a secondary source.

Watch vs Warning: A Watch means conditions are possible. A Warning means a threat has been detected or is imminent. Watches = awareness. Warnings = action.

Tornado warning polygons are intentionally larger than the tornado itself to account for uncertainty in the storm's path. Being inside a polygon does NOT mean a tornado is headed directly for you.
