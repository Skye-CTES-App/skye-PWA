Understanding Alerts

NOAA Weather Radio is the most reliable alert source — not outdoor sirens. Sirens warn people who are outside. Enable WEA (Wireless Emergency Alerts) on your phone as a backup.

Watch vs Warning: A Watch means conditions are possible. A Warning means a threat has been detected. Watches = awareness. Warnings = action.

Tornado warning polygons are intentionally larger than the tornado itself to account for path uncertainty. Being inside a polygon does NOT mean a tornado is headed directly for you.
